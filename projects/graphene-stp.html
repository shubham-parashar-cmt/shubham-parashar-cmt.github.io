Research project

Scanning tunneling potentiometry near circular graphene p–n junctions

Published collaboration: Physical Review Letters 136, 136301 (2026)

This collaboration connects nanoscale scanning tunneling potentiometry measurements in graphene to semiclassical transport around an electrostatically written circular p–n junction.

Theory role

  • Interpretation of electrochemical-potential images under applied current.
  • Semiclassical Boltzmann/Raichev-type transport calculations.
  • Connection between local electrostatics, Hall-angle physics, and observed response patterns.
