Research project

Diffusive and hydrodynamic magnetotransport near density perturbations

Published: Physical Review B 113, 155443 (2026)

This project studies current flow and electrochemical-potential response around local density perturbations in two-dimensional electron gases in strong magnetic field.

Core physics

  • No-go radius and exponential suppression of current/potential in high field.
  • Landauer resistivity dipole and its Hall-angle rotation.
  • Viscous corrections and Gurzhi-length-controlled hydrodynamic response.
