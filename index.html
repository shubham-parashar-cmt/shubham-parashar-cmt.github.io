Pathak Shubham Parashar

Researcher @ UC San Diego — hydrodynamics & quantum transport in 2D; correlated electrons; non-Hermitian & topological phases.

About

I study quantum Hall physics in higher Landau levels, magneto-transport in high magnetic fields, and itinerant phases in correlated two-dimensional systems. I also explore in-spiral black hole mergers in D > 5 dimensions.

Research

  • Hydrodynamics & Turbulence — DNS of 2+1D advection; renormalization-group scaling laws.
  • Correlated Electrons & Transport — marginal Fermi liquids; symplectic Sp(2N) Hertz–Millis; non-associative operators.
  • Non-Hermitian & Lattice Theory — extensions of the Nielsen–Ninomiya constraints.
  • Topological Phases — quantum double models in 3+1D.
  • Gravitation & HEP — black-hole inspirals (D > 5); CPV in SUSY Pati–Salam / SO(10).
