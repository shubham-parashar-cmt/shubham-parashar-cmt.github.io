About

I’m a researcher at UC San Diego focusing on quantum transport in 2D, correlated electrons, and topological phases. Current work: diffusive→ballistic crossover in graphene magnetotransport.

Previously: JAIST (MEXT Fellow), BARC (Trombay, Mumbai). I enjoy building compact theory notes, clean visualizations, and reproducible code.

Research

  • Hydrodynamics & Turbulence — DNS of 2+1D advection; renormalization-group scaling laws.
  • Correlated Electrons & Transport — marginal Fermi liquids; symplectic Sp(2N) Hertz–Millis; non-associative operators.
  • Non-Hermitian & Lattice Theory — extensions of the Nielsen–Ninomiya constraints.
  • Topological Phases — quantum double models in 3+1D.
