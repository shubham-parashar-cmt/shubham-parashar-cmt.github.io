Pathak Shubham Parashar

Researcher, UC San Diego — quantum transport in 2D; correlated electrons; topological phases.

Current focus: diffusive-to-ballistic crossover in graphene magnetotransport.

Research

  • Hydrodynamics & Turbulence — DNS of 2+1D advection; renormalization-group scaling laws.
  • Correlated Electrons & Transport — marginal Fermi liquids; symplectic Sp(2N) Hertz–Millis; non-associative operators.
  • Non-Hermitian & Lattice Theory — extensions of the Nielsen–Ninomiya constraints.
  • Topological Phases — quantum double models in 3+1D.
